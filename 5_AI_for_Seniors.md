# PROJECT BRIEF: AI Assistant Platform for Seniors

**Jim Bob Humphrey | February 2026 | Working Document**

---

## Vision

Build a voice-enabled, modular AI companion platform designed specifically for seniors navigating the final decades of life. Not an app for a task — a relationship that deepens over time, serving as a personal advisor, memory keeper, and advocate. Inspired by Jarvis (Iron Man) for capability and Alfred (Batman) for compassion.

The platform addresses a critical gap: seniors managing health, finances, legal matters, loneliness, and life transitions are expected to coordinate between multiple expensive professionals (financial advisors, attorneys, doctors) who don't communicate with each other or see the full picture. This platform becomes the integrating intelligence across all of those domains.

## Market Opportunity

- 10,000 Americans turn 65 every day. By 2030, all Baby Boomers will be 65+.
- Seniors spend $5,000–$15,000/year on professional advisory services (financial, legal, medical coordination) that don't talk to each other.
- Loneliness epidemic: AARP reports 1 in 3 adults 45+ are lonely. Social isolation increases mortality risk 26%.
- Many seniors cannot or do not want to rely on their children for financial, medical, or life planning support.
- Current AI tools (Siri, Alexa, Copilot) are task-oriented, not relationship-oriented. They have no memory, no context, no continuity.
- Jim Bob's conviction: millions of seniors are willing to pay substantially for an AI that truly knows them and helps them navigate life's most consequential decisions.

## Platform Architecture — Modular Design

The platform is modular. Users subscribe to a base platform and activate additional modules as their needs evolve. Each module deepens the AI's understanding of the user's life.

1. **Health & Medical Records** — Track medications, appointments, symptoms, lab results. Coordinate between doctors. Flag drug interactions. Prepare questions before appointments. Track cognitive changes over time. Integration with patient portals.

2. **Finance & Estate** — Monitor accounts, track spending, model retirement withdrawals, RMD planning, tax strategy. Connect with estate documents. Flag unusual transactions. The AI equivalent of tonight's $3.09M portfolio restructuring — available to everyone.

3. **Personal Safety & Wellness** — Check-ins, fall detection integration, emergency contacts, daily wellness prompts. Medication reminders. Nutrition and exercise appropriate for age and conditions.

4. **Companionship & Daily Life** — Conversation, emotional support, daily structure, hobby engagement. Not a replacement for human connection but a consistent, available presence during lonely nights and isolated days.

5. **Hobbies & Interests** — Learning, reading recommendations, travel planning, creative projects. Keeps the mind engaged and provides purpose.

6. **Family Memory Preservation** — Record stories, organize photos, capture family history, build legacy documents. Voice-recorded memories transcribed and preserved.

7. **Life & Legacy Review** — Guided life review process. Capture values, lessons, wisdom, regrets, hopes for future generations. The "Jor-El" feature — a person's accumulated wisdom preserved for those who come after.

8. **Elder Care Transition Planning** — When to consider assisted living, how to evaluate facilities, legal preparation (POA, advance directives), downsizing guidance, conversation frameworks for difficult family discussions.

9. **Final Plans & Being Remembered** — Funeral preplanning, obituary preparation, digital legacy management, final wishes documentation. Directly connected to Jim Bob's funeral service expertise.

## Key Features

### Voice-First Interface
Most seniors are more comfortable talking than typing. The platform must be primarily voice-enabled with a simple visual interface as backup.

### Persistent Memory
The AI remembers everything across sessions — health history, family relationships, financial details, personal preferences, emotional patterns, life stories. This is the core differentiator.

### Family Sharing with Guardrails
Configurable permissions for family members. Faithful daughter gets full access. Financially irresponsible son gets limited view. Inspired by 48 years of watching family dynamics around aging and death. Not all family members can be trusted with finances without guardrails.

### Blockchain-Secured Memory Vault
After years of use, the AI's accumulated knowledge of the person becomes the single most valuable element of their estate. This data is preserved using blockchain technology with pre-configured rights and permissions for who inherits access to what portions of the memory vault.

### Legacy AI ("Jor-El" Feature)
After years of living with the AI, the person's way of thinking, values, stories, and advice are captured deeply enough that future generations can interact with a version of the person's wisdom. A grandchild born after the person dies could ask, "What would Grandpa think about this?" and get a genuinely informed response.
